PRESERVAR – Cerrado Wildfire Mitigation Initiative

TRL: pilot stage

PRESERVAR is a long-term initiative focused on wildfire mitigation in the Cerrado biome, combining drones, AI models and community-based prevention systems. It aims to reduce accidental and intentional fires while generating scientific and operational knowledge.

Components

  • Integration with AERO-Q FIRE aerial monitoring
  • Risk maps and spatiotemporal analysis of fire patterns
  • Community engagement and training programs
  • Support for public policies and environmental governance
