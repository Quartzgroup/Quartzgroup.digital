IFT — Institute of Training & Technology

The IFT — Institute of Training & Technology is a Brazilian institution with 29 years of experience dedicated to education, applied science and technological development. With a trajectory marked by social commitment, academic rigor and high-quality professional training, IFT stands as the scientific and educational pillar of the Quartzgroup ecosystem.

Home / Ecosystem / IFT

Identity and Mission

IFT’s identity combines critical thinking, scientific foundations and contemporary methodologies of teaching and innovation. As the formative core of the ecosystem, IFT develops programs, research initiatives and projects integrating technology, governance, ethics and social impact — always guided by applicability and institutional responsibility.

Strategic Units Linked to IFT

  • L.I.S.I. — Laboratory of Integrated Science & Innovation, a distributed laboratory dedicated to interdisciplinary scientific research.
  • Incubatech, a digital incubator focused on technological projects and emerging initiatives.

Three Core Fronts of Action

  • (1) Technical and scientific education
  • (2) Production of applied knowledge
  • (3) Support for innovation and strategic projects within the Quartzgroup ecosystem
