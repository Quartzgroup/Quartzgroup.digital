HALO Preliminary Partnership Under NDA

Quartzgroup initiated a preliminary cooperation framework under NDA to accelerate the strategic development of HALO – Holistic Artificial Learning Operator, with a focus on its MVP roadmap and foundational cognitive architecture.

The agreement establishes a secure environment for knowledge exchange, technical evaluation and early-stage alignment between the parties involved. The collaboration includes structured discussions on data governance, AI-guided decision support, and integration with organizational strategy models.

This milestone strengthens HALO’s transition from conceptual architecture to applied validation across multiple domains, including organizational cognition, public policy analysis and territorial intelligence.
