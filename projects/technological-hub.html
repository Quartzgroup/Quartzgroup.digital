Technological Hub

TRL: planning

The Technological Hub is an integrated innovation environment designed to connect academia, industry and government through high-impact collaboration. It combines physical infrastructure, digital platforms and strategic governance to accelerate scientific and technological development.

Strategic pillars

  • Applied research and experimental platforms
  • AI, cognitive systems and advanced data analytics
  • Startup incubation and technology transfer
  • Training, reskilling and capacity building
  • Support for regional and national development agendas

Role within Quartzgroup Ecosystem

  • Anchor for collaborative research missions
  • Bridge between universities and industry
  • Environment for prototyping and innovation programs
  • Support infrastructure for HALO, H.O.L.O.S., AERO-Q and PRESERVAR initiatives
