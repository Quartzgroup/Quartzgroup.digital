Techsol Soluções em Tecnologia Ltda (Brazil)

Techsol is the applied engineering arm of the Quartzgroup ecosystem. Operating from Brazil, Techsol translates advanced concepts into concrete solutions, MVPs and deployments for public and private sector clients — bridging AI, data, infrastructure and field operations.

Home / Ecosystem / Techsol

Role in the Ecosystem Triad

While Quartzgroup defines global strategy and IFT anchors research and education, Techsol is responsible for engineering, integration and delivery. It connects laboratory, prototypes and pilots to real-world constraints, regulatory frameworks and operational environments.

Core Capabilities

  • Design and implementation of AI-enabled systems and data platforms.
  • Prototyping MVPs for HALO, AERO-Q, PRESERVAR and the Technological Hub.
  • Systems integration, infrastructure planning and deployment in critical environments.
  • Support to public agencies and companies in digital transformation and innovation projects.

From Concept to Deployment

Techsol works side by side with Quartzgroup and IFT to ensure that concepts and architectures can be validated in the field, with robust engineering, compliance and measurable impact.
