Partnerships

Quartzgroup Ecosystem develops partnerships that combine scientific research, applied innovation and education. We collaborate with institutions that share a long-term vision for responsible and high-impact use of artificial intelligence, cognitive systems and environmental intelligence.

Who we work with

We are open to collaboration with:

  • Universities and research institutes
  • Public agencies and development organizations
  • Technology companies and industrial partners
  • Innovation hubs, incubators and accelerators

Collaboration models

Depending on the context and objectives, partnerships with Quartzgroup may include:

  • Joint research projects and experimental platforms
  • Pilots and proof-of-concept initiatives in real environments
  • Technology transfer and commercialization strategies
  • Training programs, workshops and capacity building

Key domains

Our ecosystem integrates several complementary domains:

  • Artificial intelligence and cognitive systems
  • Data science and geospatial/environmental analytics
  • Organizational cognition, governance and strategic planning
  • Wildfire mitigation, territorial intelligence and sustainable technologies
