AERO-Q FIRE

TRL: 3 → 6

AERO-Q FIRE is an autonomous aerial system for wildfire detection, prediction and environmental forensics across large territories. It integrates drones, sensors, AI models and geospatial analytics to support early response and investigation.

Capabilities

  • Wide-area monitoring with autonomous aerial platforms
  • Early identification of hotspots and fire propagation risk
  • Collection of forensic data for environmental and legal analysis
  • Integration with PRESERVAR community-based prevention initiatives

Potential partners

  • Environmental agencies and fire brigades
  • Research groups in remote sensing and climate
  • Territorial management and civil defense authorities
  • Private operators in forestry, energy and infrastructure
