About Quartzgroup Ecosystem

Quartzgroup integrates research, applied innovation and education across artificial intelligence, cognitive systems and environmental intelligence.

The ecosystem is built on a triad:

  • Quartzgroup Technologies LLC – strategic holding (Delaware, USA)
  • Techsol Soluções em Tecnologia – applied engineering and innovation (Brazil)
  • IFT – Institute for Training and Technology – education and research (Brazil)

Our mission is to bridge scientific knowledge, advanced technology and human-centered solutions for global impact.
