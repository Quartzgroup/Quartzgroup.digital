About Quartzgroup Ecosystem

Quartzgroup is an integrated ecosystem that connects research, applied innovation and education across artificial intelligence, cognitive systems and environmental intelligence. Our goal is to bridge rigorous scientific thinking with high-impact, real-world applications.

The ecosystem is built on a strategic triad that operates across the full innovation chain:

  • Quartzgroup Technologies LLC – strategic holding based in Delaware (USA), responsible for global governance, international partnerships and intellectual property.
  • Techsol Soluções em Tecnologia – applied engineering and innovation company in Brazil, focused on AI, cognitive systems, geospatial analytics and sustainable technologies.
  • IFT – Institute for Training and Technology – Brazilian institute founded in 1996, dedicated to education, research and capacity building in emerging technologies.

Together, these institutions enable Quartzgroup to design and deploy solutions that combine advanced data science, intelligent systems and human-centered approaches. The ecosystem supports collaborative initiatives with universities, research institutes, public agencies and industry partners.

Our mission is to connect scientific knowledge, advanced technology and ethical governance in order to address complex societal challenges and create sustainable, future-ready outcomes.
