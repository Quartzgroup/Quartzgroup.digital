About Quartzgroup

A living ecosystem for global impact

Quartzgroup is a global innovation ecosystem designing and deploying integrated solutions in strategic governance, digital transformation, and intelligent organizational learning. Operating through Techsol (Brazil), IFT (training & research), and multiple specialized platforms, we connect academia, industry, and government to accelerate high‑impact technology with ethical AI at its core.
