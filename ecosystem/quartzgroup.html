Quartzgroup Technologies LLC (Delaware, USA)

Quartzgroup Technologies LLC is the global holding and strategic governance center of the Quartzgroup ecosystem. Incorporated in Delaware (USA), Quartzgroup provides the institutional, legal and strategic backbone that connects international partners, intellectual property, and high-impact technology initiatives.

Role in the Ecosystem Triad

As the global holding, Quartzgroup coordinates governance, long-term strategy and international positioning of the ecosystem. It aligns the efforts of Techsol (applied engineering) and IFT (research and education) with global opportunities in AI, aerospace systems, digital transformation and territorial intelligence.

Strategic Responsibilities

  • Structuring global partnerships with universities, research institutes and industry.
  • Holding and protecting intellectual property related to HALO, H.O.L.O.S., AERO-Q and other projects.
  • Defining governance models, ethical frameworks and strategic roadmaps for the ecosystem.
  • Coordinating multi-country initiatives and cross-border innovation missions.

Global Vision

Quartzgroup operates with a long-term vision: combining scientific rigor, applied intelligence and ethical responsibility to address complex challenges in public and private sectors worldwide.
