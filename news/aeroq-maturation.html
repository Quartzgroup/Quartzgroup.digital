AERO-Q FIRE & SHIELD Architecture Maturation

The AERO-Q platform entered a new phase of technical documentation and TRL elevation, advancing its FIRE and SHIELD architectures for autonomous environmental monitoring and aerial interception capabilities.

The current stage includes refinement of sensing modules, data fusion models, trajectory planning algorithms and communication interfaces with command-and-control environments.

This maturation process aligns AERO-Q with strategic partners focused on wildfire mitigation, territorial intelligence, critical asset protection and advanced robotics research.
