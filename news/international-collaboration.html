International Academic Collaboration Outlook

Quartzgroup is preparing a structured approach for collaboration with international universities and research centers across North America and Europe, with initial emphasis on AI, cognitive systems and innovation governance.

The initiative’s objective is to establish long-term, ethically grounded and high-impact partnerships capable of supporting joint research, technology development, experimental platforms and academic exchange.

The collaboration strategy will be aligned with the ecosystem’s flagship projects, including HALO, H.O.L.O.S., AERO-Q, PRESERVAR and the Technological Hub.
